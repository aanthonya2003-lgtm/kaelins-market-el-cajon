## Recommended Tier 1 photo brief (single shoot)

If the owner agrees to a single photography session, the shot list below covers every Tier 1 slot above in roughly 90 minutes on-site:

1. **Storefront exterior** — golden hour (5:30-6:30 PM in San Diego summer) — wide + medium + tight on signage
2. **Departments wide shots** (7 × 1 photo each) — Tortilleria, Carniceria, Panaderia, Produce, Hot Foods, Dulceria, Mexican & Arabic Food corner
3. **Departments details** (7 × 2 photos each = 14 photos) — counter close-ups, in-motion shots, product close-ups
4. **Hero process shots** — hands pressing tortillas / butcher trimming / pan dulce rack / pinata wall
5. **Owner portrait** — Hani Garmo behind a counter or at the storefront (only if quote also confirmed)
6. **Bonus: any vintage prints owner has from 1958-1980s** — scan or rephotograph

**Output:** ~40-50 frames, JPEG q90 + AVIF, 2400px wide, ready for `/public/` upload.
