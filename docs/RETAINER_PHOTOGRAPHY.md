# Retainer Pitch — Photography Session

> Drafted at sprint-end for delivery to the owner. Single one-time photography session that unlocks Tier 1 photography for every page on the site simultaneously.

## The current state (in one sentence)

The site is live and ranking-ready, but every product photo is a high-quality
stock placeholder — not a photo of Kaelin's actual departments, counters, or food.

## The opportunity

With one ~90-minute on-site shoot we capture every Tier 1 photo the site needs:

- 7 department wide shots
- 14 department detail shots
- 6 signature dish close-ups
- 1 storefront exterior (golden hour)
- 4 process shots (tortilla-press / butcher / panader\u00eda / pi\u00f1ata wall)
- 1 owner portrait (Hani Garmo) for the About page
- Bonus: scan any vintage 1958-era prints if available

Approx 40-50 final frames, color-corrected, exported at web-ready 2400px wide.

## Why it matters more than anything else we can build next

Reviewers consistently say Kaelin\'s **looks** like a neighborhood mercado, **feels**
like a neighborhood mercado, and **smells** like one. The website doesn't yet
communicate any of that because the photos are generic.

Replacing 21+ generic department photos with photos of the actual store will
move three needles in a way no other content change can:

1. **Bounce rate** on /departments drops sharply when shoppers see their actual
   store, their actual butcher counter, their actual pan dulce.
2. **Google Maps re-engagement** — Google Image Search regularly surfaces
   high-quality original store photos, driving local discovery.
3. **Heritage trust** — a single vintage 1958 photo is worth more for credibility
   than every other piece of copy combined. It's unforgeable.

## What we propose

**Option A: One-time photography session** (recommended first move)

- 90-minute on-site shoot, weekday afternoon
- Two photographers (one wide / one detail) or one with two cameras
- 40-50 final color-corrected frames delivered as 2400px JPEG + AVIF
- Uploaded to `/public/photos/` and wired into the site within 48 hours
- Owner approval round before publish

**Option B: Quarterly content refresh retainer** (where this becomes ongoing)

- Q1: original shoot + integration
- Q2: seasonal refresh (Cinco de Mayo, Father's Day prep)
- Q3: back-to-school / Hispanic Heritage Month
- Q4: holiday / quincea\u00f1era / New Year

Each quarterly refresh: ~15 frames, integrated within a week, paired with
2-3 SEO content posts (recipes, holiday guides) that use the photos.

## What the owner needs to decide

1. Are you open to one 90-minute on-site shoot?
2. If yes, what afternoon works in the next 2-3 weeks?
3. Comfortable with on-camera portrait for the About page? (We'll send the
   exact crop and have you approve before it ships.)
4. Are there any old prints / family photos from the early years that we can
   borrow + scan? Even one good vintage shot transforms the heritage page.

## What we need from the owner once decided

- 30-second voicemail or text confirming the quote text for /about
- Any vintage prints (we'll scan and return)
- Phone number to coordinate the shoot date

Nothing else.
